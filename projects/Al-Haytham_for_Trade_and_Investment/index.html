What areas in Oman do you serve?

We are based in Muscat and serve clients across all of Oman, including Muscat Governorate, Dhofar, Al Batinah, and other regions. We provide site visits and installations nationwide.

How long does a typical network installation take?

Implementation time varies based on project scope. Small office networks: 3-5 days. Enterprise solutions: 2-4 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during consultation and minimize business disruption.
